Our story

The 2020 founded company Wavebus combines the function of a travel service with a special community spirit We provide you with a ride to Europe'snicest surf spots.Our aim is to establish abig community for surfers all across Europe.And you are a part of it!

Whether you're driving to the sea a have a spare seat,or you're looking for a ride to Europe's nicest destinations,Wavebus is the right place to you.

It works like this: You just seat a online,so someone who want to go to the same place can book it.then he/she can easily pays the proportional price for the trip via e.g Paypal.So you're not just saving money. You will meet new cool people and listen to the playlist you never knew you needed.

Everything can happen on the trip.MAy be you need a medical support or damaged something by mistake-Our partners got you back,providing a special insurance just for our community.

This special travel insurance mainly covers health and liability matters.Nothing is more nerve-wracking than health problems or damage by mistake.And it's even more demanding when you're travelling without knowing the national conditions. This add-on will make your trip's more safely and loose.Definately.
