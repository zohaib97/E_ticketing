Germany can offer a holiday for everyone. Fly into Berlin Schonefeld Airport and explore the capital city of Germany. Here you will find stunning architecture, opera houses, theatres and plenty of divers bars, clubs and restaurants. Franz Joseph Strauss Airport serves the Munich region of Germany. From here it is a short trip to the Alps where you can enjoy fantastic skiing opportunities.

Just a two hour flight from the UK will bring you to Cologne Bonn Airport where you can experience the lively nightlife, beautiful medieval architecture, rich history and world famous beer. For a more modern city experience, why not head to Dusseldorf International Airport or Frankfurt International Airport, where you can enjoy a combination of old and new - perfect for a relaxing couple's holiday in Germany.
